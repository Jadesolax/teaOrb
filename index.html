About Orb

We are an online tea shop providing clients all over the world with high-quality and affordable teas of our own production.

  • Tea Orb has been providing different blends of herbal teas since 2018. We are producers of Lemon Balm Tea, Fennel Ginger Tea, Lemon Grass and Mint Teas and a few other herbal teas. Our relationship dates back to childhood -- when I was introduced to her by my Dad, and we fell in love. The companionship continued into college and as a result, I met other connoisseurs like me. For the purpose of freshess, I started producing herbal teas for family and friends, as far back 2015 (quite a while ago seeing we survived a Panini). but I ventured in officially, with the mind-set of building a brand in 2018, at which point I also started marketing to the larger market.
